    What is {{ g.site_title }}?

    What is the vision behind {{ g.site_title }}?

    {{ g.site_title }} is developed by the Open Knowledge Foundation Deutschland.

    {{ g.site_title }} is powered by CKAN, a data catalogue system used by various institutions and communities to manage open data. CKAN and all its components are open source software and used by a wide community of catalogue operators from across Europe, including the UK Government's data.gov.uk portal.
